MESSAGE FROM PRINCIPAL

PRINCIPAL
Dr. Milind Pandit
Principal, MSS Arts, Sci. &
Com. College, Ambad, Dist. Jalna
Dear students,
        Welcome to the Matsyodari Shikshan Sanstha's, Arts, Science & Commerce College, Ambad, Dist. Jalna.  It is a matter of great pride and contentment to be the Principal. Our parent institution 'Matsyodari Shikshan Sanstha, Jalna' is established by Adv. Ankushraoji Tope Saheb with great vision and currently running under the able guidance of Hon'ble Rajeshbhaiya Tope (Ex.Minister of Health and Family Welfare, M.S.).
        We believe that education shapes the character, calibre and prospect of the person. Education has no perimeter to restrict. We have a great history in providing high quality education catering for individual needs and preparing young people for the world of opportunities. Green and pleasant environment of the college leads to effective teaching and learning process which facilitate every students to be a better human being. Curricular and co-curricular activities are given equal importance in the college.
        As a result of imparting quality education with good infrastructure, our college is accredited with 'A' grade by NAAC. After twenty years of dedicated service to the community, we feel deep satisfaction in knowing we have touched so many lives in a positive way.
        I, on behalf of my entire team, assure you the education of the finest quality.
        Wish you best of luck for future.
